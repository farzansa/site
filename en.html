Delivery Terms & Incoterms 2020 Framework

Full transparency in international risk allocation, ocean freight expenditures, and cargo transit insurance.

FOB Delivery Terms

Free On Board - Designated Loading Hubs

Preferred by APAC Clients

Under FOB terms, Terranox undertakes all costs involving mining extraction, domestic transport, export customs clearance, and loading onto the deck of the vessel. Cargo risk transfers to the buyer once it crosses the ship's rail.

✓ Our Commitment: Inland transit, export customs clearance, vessel loading
⚡ Your Commitment: Ocean freight chartering, marine insurance, destination clearance

CIF Delivery Terms

Cost, Insurance & Freight - Destination Port

Preferred by EMEA Clients

In addition to baseline FOB liabilities, Terranox assumes payment for international ocean freight logistics to the target discharge port (e.g., Mundra or Qingdao) along with structural marine cargo insurance policies.

✓ Our Commitment: Ocean freight, transit insurance, plus complete FOB scope
⚡ Your Commitment: Port discharge costs, destination import duties & taxes
